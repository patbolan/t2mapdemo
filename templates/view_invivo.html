NLLS Fit

NLLS fitting provides the standard approach for parameter estimation, performing pixel-by-pixel curve fitting using a Non-Linear Least Squares algorithm. Note the noise characteristics in regions with varying signal-to-noise ratios.

CNN

The CNN approach was trained on synthetic data and applied to these real patient scans. The CNN generally produces smoother parameter maps with reduced noise compared to NLLS, though some fine details may be smoothed out in the process.
